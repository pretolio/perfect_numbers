Perfect Numbers

contains 2 functionalities:
1. Possibility of checking whether a number that the user enters is a PERFECT NUMBER;
2. Find all PERFECT NUMBERS between 2 given numbers in the interface by the user;
   Use whether approach you see proper to solve the problem;
